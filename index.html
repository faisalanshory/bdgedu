EQUIPOTS

Education Quality Index Map
Based On Ratio of Teacher and Student in Bandung City

See the Map See the Methods

EQUIPOTS is a platform that processes education raw-data, specifically in Bandung city, and displays it in an interactive map so that it is better understood and giving new insights so that information can be seen from a spatial perspective.

Quality Data

The informations used come
from data.bandung.go.id and Badan Pusat Statistika of Bandung city

Newest Data

So that information is still relevant to be utilized, the information selected and used is 2019 data

"Achieving inclusive and quality education for all reaffirms the belief that education is one of the most powerful and proven vehicles for sustainable development. This is also in accordance with Sustainable Development Goal number 4: "Quality Education". To support this goal, EQUIPOTS tries to approach the calculation of the quality of education through a spatial approach based on comparative information on the number of teachers and student"

Features

Student to Teacher Ratio

Information about the value of comparison between the number of teachers and students in three levels of education at the district level in Bandung

Education Quality Index

The education quality index value for each district in Bandung is calculated by a certain method based on the ratio of student to teacher ratio and the pure education participation rate

Interactive Web Maps

Information is displayed in a web-based interactive map based on real location so that it is easy to use and understand.
